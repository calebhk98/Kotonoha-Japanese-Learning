## Notes

- **N5 songs** feature simple, repetitive vocabulary and slow pacing—ideal for absolute beginners
- **N5-N4 transition songs** introduce narrative elements and slightly more varied vocabulary while remaining accessible
- **All songs** are authentic Japanese originals (no translations of English songs)
- **Availability:** All listed songs can be found on YouTube; lyrics will be sourced from official or verified Japanese lyrics sites (AZLyrics, Genius Japan, etc.)
- **Future additions** will expand into more advanced categories (N4, N3+) with progressively complex themes and vocabulary
